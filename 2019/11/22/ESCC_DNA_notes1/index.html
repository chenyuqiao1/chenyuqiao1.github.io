方法学

  1. 测序

自己只有9个厦门中山医院的数据。

  1. Somatic mutation analysis and genotyping

302个数据，BWA进行比对，Picard mark duplicate ，VarScan2 帅选somatic and germline 突变，阈值mapping quality 大于30，coverage 大于10。得到的突变进一步筛选标准，allele depth大于10。

此外，还使用了GATK Haploytype Caller call germline 突变。

germline突变取交集。

为了确定胚系突变的基因型，筛选标准是，mapping quality 大于30，coverage of alternate 大于5，total coverage 大于20.

We then determined the genotype based on alternate allele coverage rate (ACR) as the followings: (1)
ACR ≤ 0.1, homozygote of the reference allele; (2) 0.2 ≤ ACR ≤ 0.8, heterozygote; (3) ACR ≥ 0.9, homozygote of the alternate allele.

也即是coverage of alternate/ total coverage

此外

CHB: Han Chinese in Beijing;

CHS: Southern Han Chinese;

TGP: The 1000 Genomes Project;

Population stratification of 302 ESCC patients. Two hundred eight genotyped reference individuals are
obtained from TGP including 103 CHB and 105 CHS. After filtering 20 outliers, the remaining 282 samples are classified into CHB and CHS at a threshold level of 0 for PC2.

也就是将千人计划中的103 北京汉族人口和105南方汉族的汉族人口和分析的302的食管癌患者，总共510个患者的germline mutation 进行PCA分析。

  1. somatic mutational signature

使用pmsignature 这个R包对mutation的signature和COSMIC 数据库做对比分析

  1. somatic CNV

同样使用的是Varscan2这个软件，筛选标准是coverage 大于20，base quality 大于20，mapping quality 大于20。

The breakpoints were defined based on significant change of the ratio-of-depth between the tumor and the matched normal (p < 0.05 with Fisher’s Exact Test).

We obtained the autosomal segments of copy-number changes using circular binary segmentation (CBS) and determined the somatic copy-number status of each segment by the log2-adjusted ratio-of-depth.

gain 是指的是大于0.5，lose指的是这个值小于-0.5

JISTIC was used to find the significant regions and genes of SCNAs [50]

这是一款注释软件；

软件下载地址https://systemsbiology.columbia.edu/center-for-computational-biology-and-bioinformatics-c2b2

  1. 关于突变基因的注释

The somatic mutations were annotated using Oncotator (v1.5.1.0) [51].

Then we evaluated the enrichment of the non-synonymous somatic mutations (SMGs are FDR < 0.1) in each gene using MutSigCV (v1.4) [52].

We analyzed the pathways of genes that are either significantly frequently mutated (MutSigCV p < 0.05) and strongly associated with SCNAs using HotNet2 [53].

这里用到了三个软件，第一个是Oncotator 这个软件进行注

MutSigCV 这个软件最显著突变的寻找

HotNet2 用来寻找最显著突变且和SCNA相关的gene的通路。

这部分关于注释的方法学，还是值得参考的
